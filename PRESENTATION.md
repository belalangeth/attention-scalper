# Attention Scalper: Quant-Driven Social Distribution

## The Problem
Most hackathon projects fail not because of bad code, but because of zero distribution. Founders spend 99% of their time building and 1% shouting into the void on X. Manual social media management is slow, inconsistent, and often filled with generic "AI slop" that the market immediately ignores.

## The Solution
Attention Scalper is an execution-focused narrative engine that treats social media as a quantifiable market. It removes the guesswork from project launches by providing a deterministic, 4-week execution playbook based on the project's specific technical architecture.

## Key Features
- **Narrative Routing:** Automatically calibrates tone based on tech stack (e.g., Infrastructure vs. Consumer).
- **120-Second Genesis Logic:** High-precision entry strategies for the first window of market attention.
- **Structured JSON Output:** Clean, parseable data designed to be integrated into any dashboard or frontend.
- **Enterprise-Grade Security:** Built-in rate limiting, helmet protection, and strict payload validation to prevent abuse.

## Technical Architecture
- **Engine:** Built with Node.js and TypeScript.
- **Inference:** Powered by OpenRouter (Llama 3.3 70B) for high-reasoning output without the marketing fluff.
- **Deployment:** Fully Dockerized for instant scaling and deployment.

## The Pitch
Distribution shouldn't be a creative mystery. It should be a protocol. Attention Scalper turns social presence into a deterministic execution layer for the next generation of Solana founders.
